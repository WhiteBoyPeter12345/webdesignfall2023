Kyle "BUGHA" Giersdorf



Kyle Giersdorf is a 20 year old American professional Esports gamer and competitor better known by his gaming moniker, Bugha. His best game is Fortnite as he has the most skill in that and he became well-known after winning the 2019 Fortnite World Cup.
